Working and USES

The main objective behid building this Project named Music Speaks is to allow people to feel good and happy by playing music. They can enjoy playing music while they are in quarantine and boost up their skills in piano too. It is just a prototype but we are optimistic that we can take this to next level. You might feel why people in quarantine needs this, they may watch tv, play games and so on but we are focusing those peole who are sitting on quarantine where they have no facilities and they are getting bored. We have also planned to make an platform where two or more friends can play same song watching music sheets and help each other learn the skills of piano in this global pandemic while sitting at quarantine. We are sure that Music can definitely help in improving ones mental health so our project can be considered as fun yet social welfare project. :-) :-)
